医院介绍 / Hospital Introduction

中山大学肿瘤防治中心（中山大学附属肿瘤医院、中山大学肿瘤研究所）成立于1964年3月，是新中国成立最早的四所肿瘤医院之一。中心是全国规模最大、学术力量最雄厚的集医疗、教学、科研、预防于一体的肿瘤学基地之一，承担国家肿瘤防治重任，在全国尤其是华南地区及港澳台的肿瘤防治工作中发挥着龙头作用，学科地位、综合实力居全国领先水平。

Sun Yat-sen University Cancer Center (SYSUCC), also known as The Affiliated Cancer Hospital and Institute of Sun Yat-sen University, was established in March 1964. It is one of the earliest four cancer hospitals founded in New China. As one of the largest and most academically robust oncology bases in China integrating medical care, teaching, research, and prevention, SYSUCC shoulders the significant national responsibility of cancer prevention and treatment. It plays a leading role in cancer control across the nation, particularly in South China, Hong Kong, Macao, and Taiwan, with its disciplinary status and comprehensive strength ranking among the top tier in the country.

中心主办英文学术期刊《Cancer Communications》（《癌症通讯》），该刊多次入选"百种中国杰出学术期刊"和"中国最具国际影响力学术期刊"；2014年被科学引文索引（SCI）收录，目前影响因子为24.9（JCR 2024），在JCR肿瘤学分类中位于Q1区，在亚洲综合肿瘤学领域学术期刊中排名第一。

The center publishes the English academic journal *Cancer Communications*, which has been repeatedly selected as one of the "Top 100 Outstanding Chinese Academic Journals" and "China's Most Influential International Academic Journals." It was indexed by Science Citation Index (SCI) in 2014 and currently holds an Impact Factor of 24.9 (JCR 2024). It ranks in the Q1 quadrant in the JCR oncology category and is the No. 1 academic journal in the field of comprehensive oncology in Asia.

结直肠外科介绍 / Introduction to the Department of Colorectal Surgery

结直肠外科成立于2009年，其前身为腹科的一部分。在万德森教授和潘志忠主任的领导下，全科上下齐心协力，取得了显著成就。

The Department of Colorectal Surgery was established in 2009, which used to be part of the Department of Abdominal Surgery. Under the leadership of Professor Desen Wan and Director Zhizhong Pan, the whole department has made remarkable achievements with concerted efforts.

该科目前设有两个病区，共70张床位，并拥有造口治疗室、肛门直肠测压室和研究实验室。目前，它已成为中国最大、最具影响力的结直肠癌医疗中心之一。

The department now has two sections, 70 beds, an ostomy treatment room, an anorectal manometry room, and a research laboratory. At present, it has become one of the largest and most influential medical centers for colorectal cancer in China.
